About Our Restaurant

Exceptional Food, Great Prices and Friendly People.

Dave's is Steveston's Original and Best Fish & Chips serving exceptional food, at great prices with friendly people for over 30 years since 1978. We offer great seafood in the village of Steveston, a village within the city of Richmond. Dave's fish includes Salmon, Halibut, Cod, Shrimp, all cooked for your enjoyment. With the pride of winning continuous years of Best Fish and Chips in Richmond, we know you will love it!
